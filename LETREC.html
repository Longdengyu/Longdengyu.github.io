LETREC: A Language with Recursive Procedures¶

LETREC 语言增加了递归函数，语法如下：

 1"""
 2    syntax:
 3    Program ::= Expression
 4            a-program(exp1)
 5
 6    Expression ::= Number
 7                const-exp(num)
 8
 9    Expression ::= - (Expression, Expression)
10                diff-exp(exp1, exp2)
11
12    Expression ::= zero? (Expression)
13                zero?-exp(exp1)
14
15    Expression ::= if Expression then Expression else Expression
16                if-exp(exp1, exp2, exp3)
17
18    Expression ::= Identifier
19                var-exp(var)
20
21    Expression ::= let Identifier = Expression in Expression
22                let-exp (var, exp1, body)
23
24    Expression ::= proc (Identifier) Expression
25                proc-exp (var, body)
26
27    Expression ::= (rator, rand)
28                call-exp(rator, rand)
29
30    Expression ::= letrec Identifier (Identifier) = Expression in Expression
31                letrec-exp (p-name b-var p-body letrec-body)
32"""

重点在LetRecEnv，根据letrec的语义，如果apply的这个递归函数的name，那么就构造一个 procedure给它，否则用往老的env里面查。相关逻辑如下：

 1class LetRecEnv(Env):
 2    def __init__(self, p_name, b_var, p_body, saved_env: Env):
 3        self.p_name = p_name
 4        self.b_var = b_var
 5        self.p_body = p_body
 6        self.saved_env = saved_env
 7
 8    def apply(self, var):
 9        if var == self.p_name:
10            return ProcVal(self.b_var, self.p_body, self)
11        else:
12            return self.saved_env.apply(var)

核心代码其实就在apply这个函数里面
